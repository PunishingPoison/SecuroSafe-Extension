Securo/Safe Settings

When enabled, Securo/Safe will automatically use Google Safe Browsing to check websites you visit for known threats. A warning icon will appear if a potential risk is found.

Privacy

Securo/Safe is designed with your privacy in mind.

  • The automatic scan only sends the URL you visit to Google's Safe Browsing service. No other data is sent.
  • The on-demand fact-check only sends the text or URL you explicitly enter in the popup to our analysis service.
  • We do not store or track your browsing history.
